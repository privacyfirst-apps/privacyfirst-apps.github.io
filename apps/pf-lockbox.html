PF LockBox

Version 1.0.0 • Released 2025

PF LockBox is a fully offline password vault built for people who want strong security without cloud dependency. It lets you protect all your credentials behind a master password, organize entries with categories, generate strong passwords, store secure notes, and export encrypted backups. Everything stays on your device, with PrivacyFirst Apps branding and a clean desktop experience focused on privacy, control, and simplicity.

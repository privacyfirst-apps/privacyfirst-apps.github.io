PF NoteVault

Version 1.0.0 • Released 2025

PF NoteVault is an offline desktop note manager built for users who want productivity without sacrificing privacy. It lets you create rich notes, organize them with categories and tags, mark favorites, archive or restore notes, and export them in useful formats like TXT and PDF. All data stays on your computer, making it a true PrivacyFirst Apps experience with no cloud dependency.

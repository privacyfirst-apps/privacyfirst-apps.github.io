PF Typing Studio

Version 1.0.0 • Released 2025

PF Typing Studio is a modern typing practice app designed to help users improve speed, accuracy, and consistency. It offers customizable typing tests with options like time or word mode, punctuation, numbers, difficulty levels, and content types such as English words, quotes, code, and custom text. The app also includes performance stats, achievements, shortcuts, and both light and dark themes for a comfortable typing experience.

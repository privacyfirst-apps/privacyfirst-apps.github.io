About the Brand

Why PrivacyFirst Apps Exists

We started PrivacyFirst Apps because we were tired of the same pattern everywhere: download a free app, and it's loaded with ads, trackers, and telemetry that phones home to servers you've never heard of.

We believe software should be a tool that serves you, not a pipeline that extracts your data. Every app we build follows one simple philosophy:

"Your computer, your data, your business. We just build the tools."

Our apps are built with Electron, packaged as native Windows .exe files, and designed to work completely offline. When we say no data collection, we mean it — our apps don't even have the code to send data anywhere.
